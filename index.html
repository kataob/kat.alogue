Profile

Power systems engineer with five years of experience across renewable energy project development, grid integration, and optimization-based design of hybrid wind-solar-battery power plants. Through a PhD at the Technical University of Denmark, an applied research stay at TotalEnergies, and prior project development work at RP Global, I combine feasibility studies, techno-economic modeling, and owner-side technical support to bridge system design with real-world project delivery — with a particular commitment to supporting renewable energy scale-up in emerging markets.
